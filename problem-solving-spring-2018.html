Problem Solving training(spring 2018)



About

Our first movement, where we first established the club and then started the training immediately, basically it has included basic subjects in competitive programming and in C++ language that helps to solve mathematical and computational problems. We then started with some basic algorithms related to searching. Each week included 2 classes with 2 hours each and was presented by our team.


Prerequisites

  • Good knowledge about C++ basics; i.e. input/output , for loops , arrays .
  • Codeforces Registration
  • Joining Our university united group
  • Make sure to join our club group page on facebook
