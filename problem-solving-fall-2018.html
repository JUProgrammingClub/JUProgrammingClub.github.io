Problem Solving training(fall 2018)



About

This semester we are giving a beginner problem solving training that basically has included basic subjects in competitive programming and in C++ language that helps to solve mathematical and computational problems. We then started with some basic algorithms related to searching. Each week included 3 classes with 1 hour each and was presented by our team.


Prerequisites

  • Good knowledge about C++ basics; i.e. input/output , for loops , arrays .
  • Codeforces Registration
  • Joining Our university united group
  • Make sure to join our club group page on facebook
