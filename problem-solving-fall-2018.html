Problem Solving training(fall 2018)



About

This semester we are giving a beginner problem solving training that basically includes basic subjects in competitive programming and in C++ language that helps to solve mathematical and computational problems. This trainig contests of 3 hours a week as 1 hour for 3 days. Sunday and Tuesday have 2 lectures that explain the material of the week and Thursday has 1 hour in the lab where we solve problems about the material explained before.


Prerequisites

  • Good knowledge about C++ basics; i.e. input/output , for loops , arrays .
  • Codeforces Registration
  • Joining Our university united group
  • Make sure to join our club group page on facebook
