Welcome to JU Programming Club


About

Our club was established in 2018 as a non-profit student initiative with support from the amazing professors in the computer engineering department at university of Jordan. Its goal was to engage students in learning and enjoying programming together with many diffrent and interactive activities.

We don't only love programming ,but drink, eat and breath it too. If you ask any of us about our calendar you'll find it busy with projects, onsite and online contests and much of team working to improve ourselves as much as we can. Our club is the best place for people who are interested in all programming related subjects.

Our expected goals are:

  • To improve our technical skills and prepare ourselves for the jobs and the labor market.
  • To increase the interaction and collaboration between students and help each other to grow a better community of programmers.
  • To raise our algorithmic and problem solving skills to participate in the regional and international contests like ICPC , IEEEXtreme ... etc.
  • Increase the students knowledge of open source and how to contribute to it.
  • Make workshops that talk about famous and important websites and programs for programmers like Github, LinkedIn and more.
  • Discuss important technological affairs and issues and their latest updates.

Activities

Our team in the club provides engaging activites every semester to keep our people in the club in a circle of learning new things in the university and apply them in our trainings after. Some of our actvites in previous semesters are :

  • Problem Solving training(fall 2018).
  • Problem Solving training(spring 2018).
  • Java training(spring 2018).
  • JUCPC Contest preparations and organization (spring 2018).
  • JU Mirror Contest (summer 2018).
